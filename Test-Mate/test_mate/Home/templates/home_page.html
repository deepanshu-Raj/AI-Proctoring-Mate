Hi, I'm TestMate, an AI-based proctoring
software to help teachers take online exams

In these new times, taking online tests and preventing cheating in them has become a huge challenge for the teachers.
Thus we wanted to develop an AI based proctoring which is not only easy to usenfor teachers less familiar with computers,
but also makes an improvement over the existing AI Proctors

Why our AI is better?

A glimpse of our AI.

Our aim while developing this project was to think like a candidate and the ways in which a candidate could cheat and then coming up with measures to counter them without use of any other external equipment.
Our AI uses a combination of strong image processing algorithms as well as sound processing algorithms to effectively detect cheating.
